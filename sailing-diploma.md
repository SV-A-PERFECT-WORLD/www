# Pretty Good Crew Diploma

Syllabus:

# Meteorology based on Windy

- looking at big picture situation 

  with pressure isolines, identifygin lows and highs,

- comparing weather models

  detecting contradictions


## Prepare for Sailing

- secure galley and cabins

- switch on instruments

- unlock rudder, tension steering lines, test hard-over

- prepare foresail

- lower, activate and test engines


## Lifting Anchor

- take in first meters until hook

- remove bridal hook

- lift remaining anchor until almost vertical

- break anchor and watch drift

- store anchor

## Begin Sailing

- engage engine power (a bit)

- turn into desired course

- deploy foresail

- check speed and course

- stop engine


## Anchoring

- pick anchor location

- approach anchor location under sail

- turn into wind

- roll-reef foresail

- engage engine power (a bit)

- drop anchor 

- observe drift and swinging

- pay out more anchor chaino

- hook bridal into chain

- observe final situation


## Sorting the boat

- clean foresheet and roll-reef line

- lift engines

- switch off instruments

- lock rudder
