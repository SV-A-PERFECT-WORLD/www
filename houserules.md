## Values

1. One-ness (with nature and with each other)
1. Sustainability
1. Kindness

## Principles

1. we travel with the wind - ABSOLUTELY NO DEADLINES
1. we create synergy by helping each other
1. we create synergy by cooking for each other
1. zero food waste

1. don't create traps
1. don't step on ropes
1. coil or store ropes when not in use
1. sort everything back into its place after use
1. clean dishes in the moment when you stop using them
1. use water responsibly
1. use energy responsibly


## House Rules

1. be sceptical and critical about every rule, but respect them until we negotiated new rules
1. speak your mind, challenge any rule and make a better suggestion
1. ventilate cabins sufficiently (weather dependent)
1. store matress vertically while not sleeping (so that it does not rot)
1. wipe cabin walls clean from condensation (remove humidity)
1. wipe cabin floor clean from water (so that we can monitor water intake)
1. clean dishes with help of silicone spoon and hot water - drink it
1. don't use soap for dishes unless absolutely necessary
1. on board we keep only food that is vegan + organic
1. we keep and eat food only in the galley or eat on deck
1. no private food stashes
1. no eating in cabins
1. no alcohol (or other drugs) during cruises - only when moored
1. use outdoor toilet or go to land
1. en-suite toilet only for absolute emergencies
1. bring all your personal stuff (life vest, clothes, snacks...) for your shift to the bridge (no visit to cabin should be necessary)
1. start a shift with all your biological needs already served (no breaks for toilet or food shall be necessary, however snacking is OK)
1. come in time for all appointments (shifts...) or earlier
